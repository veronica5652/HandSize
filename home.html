Welcome to the digit ratio application!

Your digit ratio is the ratio between the length of your pointer and ring fingers. Several studies have found correlations between this ratio and different traits such as autism, homosexuality, aggression, and sports aptitude.

The application should be used for entertainment purposes only. It is not intended to diagnose or treat any medical conditions. If you think you might have a medical condition, you should consult your doctor.
